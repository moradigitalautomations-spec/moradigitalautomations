About Mora Digital Automations

We help small shops and restaurants adopt practical automation — no jargon, just results. Founder-led, local-first and built to be maintainable.

Founded in Puducherry by Mohanarangan, Mora focuses on simple, reliable automation: WhatsApp workflows, n8n integrations and lightweight CRM setups. Our aim is to save owners time and increase revenue through pragmatic automation.

  • Mission: Make automation accessible, affordable and reliable for every local business.
  • Vision: Local shops that scale with simple, dependable automation.
  • What we build: WhatsApp bots, n8n flows, lead capture, simple dashboards and supplier automations.
  • How we work: Discover (we study operations) → Build (practical workflows) → Deliver (training + maintenance).
  • Support: Clear maintenance plans, rapid local support and transparent pricing.

If you want a focused automation review for your shop or restaurant, use the contact page — we'll reply via WhatsApp with a clear plan.

— Mohanarangan (Founder)
