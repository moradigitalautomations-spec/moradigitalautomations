About Mora Digital Automations

Mora Digital Automations helps small shops and restaurants adopt practical automation — no jargon, just results.

Founded in Puducherry by Mohanarangan, Mora focuses on simple, reliable automation: WhatsApp workflows, n8n integrations, and lightweight CRM setups. The goal is to let small business owners focus on customers, not repetitive tasks.

  • Mission: Make automation accessible, affordable and reliable for every local business.
  • Vision: A future where local shops use smart workflows to grow their sales and save time.
  • Values: Local-first, practical, transparent, founder-led support.
  • Process: Discover → Build → Deliver. We analyze operations, build tailored workflows, and train the team.
  • Support: Simple maintenance plans — automated systems that keep running with minimal upkeep.

If you'd like to discuss a specific automation for your shop or restaurant, book a free consultation from the contact page — we’ll respond via WhatsApp.
