About the Founder — Mohanarangan

Mohanarangan founded Mora Digital Automations in Puducherry with a simple goal — make reliable automation affordable for local shops and restaurants. He blends practical engineering with the realities of small-business operations so solutions are effective and easy to maintain.

With experience building small automation stacks, Mohanarangan focuses on no-nonsense implementations: WhatsApp menus for orders, n8n flows for data sync, and simple CRMs to capture and follow up leads. Mora is built around real-world results and local support.

Mission

Bring straightforward, cost-effective automation to businesses that need it most: local shops and restaurants that want more time and more sales without complicated software bills.

Values

  • Practicality over complexity
  • Local-first support
  • Transparent pricing
